Hello! My name is Patrick, and I am currently a student in the University of Washington's Coding Bootcamp, working on becoming a full stack web developer. I've done everything from working as a massage therapist & energy worker, to working in nightclubs. I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree, and have experience in ceramics, woodworking, painting, metal fabrication, and glass tube bending. I make sculptures out of neon lights. I am an artist. I've also been a manager, a teacher, and now...a web developer!


I love everything I am learning, and am looking forward to seeing how I will be able to contribute to this industry. I will keep adding web-design and coding projects to this site as I continue my journey in web-development. For now, I've included some of my previous creative endeavors. Enjoy!
